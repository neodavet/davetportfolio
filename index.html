About Me

I am a Senior Full Stack Web Developer and Project Manager with over a decade of experience driving digital transformation across diverse industries, including technology, e-commerce, and digital marketing. Throughout my career, I have successfully led cross-functional teams and managed the end-to-end delivery of over 100 web and software development projects. My expertise spans operations management, product strategy, team leadership, client relations, and Agile methodologies such as Scrum. I bring a strategic mindset to every challenge, ensuring on-time, high-quality deliverables that align with business goals. I thrive in remote, multicultural environments and am skilled at fostering collaboration across geographically dispersed teams.

My technical proficiency includes web development, performance optimization, web accessibility, and cybersecurity, along with deep knowledge of SEO/SEM and marketing automation. I have hands-on experience with a wide range of tools and technologies, including PHP, JavaScript, WordPress, Laravel, NodeJS, and cloud platforms like AWS and Google Cloud. I am certified in Scrum Foundations, Remote Work, and currently pursuing certifications as a Product Owner, Scrum Master, and Agile Leader. Whether developing scalable platforms or managing enterprise-level initiatives, I am committed to delivering solutions that drive business impact and user value.
